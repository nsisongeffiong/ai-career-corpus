# Project Portfolio — Outside Employment

Projects, ventures, and initiatives that aren't tied to a paid role but show capability, drive, and range. Especially useful for senior/leadership applications where "what you build on your own time" matters.

**Include:**
- Side businesses and ventures
- Open-source contributions
- Major personal projects (sites, blogs, podcasts)
- Volunteer/community roles
- Partnerships and unrealized ventures — concept work counts, *if framed honestly*

---

## Stage and framing guide

Use the **Status** field below to keep AI-generated bullets honest. Pick from:

- **Shipped & ran** — launched, served customers/users, generated revenue or measurable usage
- **Built, not launched** — working prototype/MVP exists, didn't get to market
- **Pitched, not built** — proposal/business case authored, pitched to partners or investors
- **Concept-only** — strategic thinking on paper; market research, partnership outreach, business modeling — no build
- **Paused / abandoned** — started something, did not finish

### Framing verbs by stage

| Stage | Use these verbs | Never use |
|---|---|---|
| Shipped & ran | Launched, Operated, Grew, Scaled, Served | — |
| Built, not launched | Built, Prototyped, Developed, Designed (MVP) | Launched, Ran |
| Pitched, not built | Conceived, Proposed, Designed, Pitched, Researched, Modeled | Built, Launched |
| Concept-only | Conceived, Scoped, Researched, Authored proposal for | Built, Launched, Ran |
| Paused / abandoned | Initiated, Began work on | Anything implying completion |

Concept-only work is still valuable evidence of strategic thinking, market research, and business-case writing. Frame it that way — don't oversell it.

### Trailhead (hiking-log app)
**2024 – 2025  |  Sole developer**
**Status:** Built, not launched

- **What it was:** A mobile app for logging hikes offline and syncing route data when back in coverage.
- **Why it mattered:** Existing trackers assumed connectivity; back-country hikers lose their logs.
- **What you did:** Designed the offline-first sync model, built a working prototype (React Native + SQLite), tested with 5 friends over one season.
- **Outcome:** Prototype worked; shelved when a major competitor shipped offline mode. Lesson: validate the moat before building the second feature.
- **Tech stack / skills:** `React Native, SQLite, TypeScript`
- **Tags:** `[#mobile, #offline-first, #product-design, #built-not-launched]`
