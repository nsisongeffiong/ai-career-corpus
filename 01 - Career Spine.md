# Career Spine — Chronological Record

Every role you've held, with full context. This is the canonical timeline. When AI generates a resume, it draws context and achievements from here.

**Conventions:**
- Newest role at top
- Each achievement uses **Context → Action → Result** structure
- Tag every achievement with skills `[#skill]` and tech `[#tech]` so AIs can filter
- Quantify everything you can — `25%`, `$50k`, `8 hours/week`, `from 4 to 12 engineers`, `99.9% uptime`
- If you can't quantify, describe scope — "across 3 business units", "for a Fortune 500 client"
- Mark unverified details `*[verify]*` rather than guessing — the corpus must be factual

### Platform Engineer — Contoso Logistics
**March 2022 – Present  |  Springfield (Remote)**

- **Reported to:** Director of Platform Engineering
- **Team size:** 6 peers, no direct reports
- **Mandate:** Modernize a legacy deployment process that was blocking weekly releases; bring infrastructure under code.

**Achievements:**

- **Deployment automation:** Releases required a 4-hour manual runbook and failed ~1 in 5 attempts. Built a CI/CD pipeline (GitHub Actions + Terraform) with automated rollback. **Cut release time to 20 minutes and failure rate below 2%.** `[#cicd, #terraform, #github-actions, #automation]`
- **Cost reduction:** Audited cloud spend across three environments; rightsized instances and moved batch workloads to spot capacity. **Saved $8,000/month (22%).** `[#cloud, #finops, #cost-savings]`
- **Mentorship:** Onboarded two junior engineers onto the platform team — paired weekly, designed a graduated on-call ladder. Both reached independent on-call within one quarter. `[#mentorship, #onboarding]`

**Tech stack:** `AWS, Terraform, GitHub Actions, Docker, Python, Bash`
**Notable stakeholders / clients:** Release engineering, finance (cloud budget), Fabrikam (enterprise client)
**What I learned / how I grew:** The diagnostic month before automating anything is what made the automation land — mapping the failure modes first meant automating the right steps, not just the visible ones.
